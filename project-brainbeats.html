Brain Beats

Brain Beats is an innovative AI-powered web application designed to help students study more effectively by turning their notes into catchy songs. I led the frontend development and collaborated with a team integrating GPT-Neo for lyrics and Magenta for music synthesis.

  • Tech Used: Python, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GPT-Neo, Magenta
  • Challenges: Integrating ML models with frontend, optimizing note parsing
  • Outcome: Working prototype deployed for demo, received positive feedback
